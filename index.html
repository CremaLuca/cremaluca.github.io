Replicated and distributed networks based on SMS communication

Android + Java ~ from 12/2019 to 02/2020 ~ Together with Marco Mariotto, Marco Cognolato and Enrico Cestaro

After a research about replicated and distributed network this library has been produced. We implemented it using SMSs but interfaces are suitable for an internet based network too. Two kind of network has been implemented: replicated and distributed (Kademlia). Both kinds allow setting a resource, getting it and removing it.

RingUp

Android + Java ~ from 10/2019 to 12/2019 ~ Together with Alberto Ursino and Marco Mariotto

As part of a university course we developed an application based on SMSs to make your phone ring by sending it a code and a password. Very useful to find your phone when you forgot to turn connection on.

SMS library

Android + Java ~ from 10/2019 to 11/2019 ~ Together with Marco Mariotto, Alberto Ursino, Marco Cognolato and Matteo Carnelos

Android library to send and receive SMS messages quickly. Encodes a hidden character in messages in order to hide them from the rest of the applications. Looks small but it's been a difficult way to have 20 people agree on the same structure.

Nuotobet

PHP + jQuery + Ajax ~ from 01/2015 to 03/2015 and from 03/2019 to 06/2019 ~ Thanks to Giacomo Vettoretti and Marco Esposito

The idea started as a joke, a place where swimmers and their friends could bet on the competitions results; then it became the first webapp I had ever developed. It also gives possibility to see athlete statistics over time.
I'll come back and finish it properly one day.

Zero/Nullo

Unity3D + C# ~ from 02/2018 to 08/2018 ~ Thanks to Igor Zanella and Oleksandr Demian

The idea came from a friend of mine: a game where you have to make a number reach 0 by using operations in the right order. It has been a hard first approach to project management for a team of three and the last approach to games developement so far.

Swims

Unity3D + Javascript ~ from 04/2015 to 06/2015

My first approach to game developement and to Unity3D. A gamed that gained its popularity as quick as it lost it, a clicker based on swimming where you had to click faster than AI to win competitions. It became more interesting when results were compared using Google Play Services leaderboards.

Rivendilibro

PHP + JQuery ~ from 05/2016 to 06/2016
